Role: Stakeholder
Customers who will supply requirements for the product, and verify the satisfaction of those requirements through testing of released builds.
Relationships
Primary Performs
  • Execute User Acceptance Tests
  • Requirements Validation
  • Validate Preliminary Builds with Stakeholders
Additionally Performs
  • Gather Requirements
  • Requirements Elicitation Meeting
Modifies
  • Approval for Implemented Version
  • Approval to Begin Development
  • Partially Functional Build
  • User Acceptance Test Feedback
